    About Bates Stamping

    What is Bates Stamping?

    Bates stamping (also called Bates numbering) is the process of applying unique, sequential identification numbers to legal documents and evidence. Originally developed by the Bates Manufacturing Company in the late 1800s, this system has become the gold standard for organizing discovery documents, depositions, and trial exhibits in legal proceedings.
