Our Mission

Free, private legal document tools for everyone. BatesStamp.com gives attorneys, paralegals, and anyone handling legal documents a fast, free, and completely private toolkit — without uploading sensitive files to any server.

About This Toolkit

BatesStamp.com was built out of frustration with expensive, clunky document management software that charged subscription fees for tasks that are ultimately straightforward. Solo practitioners and smaller firms shouldn't have to pay for enterprise software just to number documents, redact privilege, or generate pleading paper.

Every tool processes documents directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDFs never leave your device — there is no backend, no cloud storage, and no server to breach.

🔒 Your documents are processed 100% locally. No files are uploaded. No data is stored. Works offline once the page loads.

What You Can Do

Document Assembly

Assemble multi-document filing packages by combining, reordering, and merging PDFs with the Filing Assembler.

Bates Stamping

Add sequential Bates numbers, exhibit labels, and confidentiality notices to PDF documents with full customization.

Privilege Redaction

Permanently redact privileged or sensitive content from PDFs using rasterization to ensure complete removal.

PDF Tools

Strip metadata, extract specific pages, compress file size, and add watermarks — all in your browser.

Pleading Paper Generator

Generate California-style pleading paper with line numbers, case caption, and court-ready formatting.

PFS Calculator

Calculate Florida Statute § 768.79 Proposal for Settlement thresholds at pfscalculator.com.
