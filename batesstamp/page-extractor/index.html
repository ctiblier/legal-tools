About PDF Page Extraction

What is PDF Page Extraction?

PDF page extraction lets you pull specific pages from a larger document and save them as a new, standalone PDF. Instead of sharing an entire document when only a few pages are relevant, you can produce a clean excerpt — exactly the pages your recipient needs.

Why Use Our Free PDF Page Extractor?

Our tool renders visual thumbnails of every page so you can see exactly what you are selecting. You can also type a page range directly — the two methods stay in sync. All extraction happens entirely in your browser with no server uploads, making it safe for privileged documents and confidential materials.

Visual Selection

Click thumbnails to select or deselect individual pages. A blue border and counter confirm your selection before you extract.

Range Input

Type ranges like "1-3, 7, 12-15" to select pages by number. The thumbnail grid updates instantly to match.

Privacy First

Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser. No file is ever uploaded to any server — suitable for attorney-client materials and confidential exhibits.

Common Uses in Legal Practice

  • Filing excerpts: Extract only the relevant pages of a long document for inclusion in a brief or motion
  • Partial exhibits: Produce a subset of a larger exhibit record without redacting the rest
  • Deposition excerpts: Pull specific transcript pages for use in supporting motions
  • Correspondence excerpts: Extract individual letters or attachments from a multi-document PDF
  • Contract sections: Isolate specific clauses or schedules for quick reference
